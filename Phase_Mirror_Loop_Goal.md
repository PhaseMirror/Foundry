Goal: Phase Mirror Loop:

1. Analyze goals, claims, math, policy, constraints...

2. Identify tensions between stated intent and operating incentives, urgency and capacity, risk claimed and risk owned, and control desired versus available.  

3. Rank these tensions by impact and tractability.  

4. Produce an ADR plan to address the tensions. 

5. Then commence production grade resolve the dissonance.
