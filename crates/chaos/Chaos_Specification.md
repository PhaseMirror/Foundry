# chaOS Specification

## Purpose
`chaos` (chaOS-RS) provides the core stochastic engines and poly-ontological resonance frameworks for PhaseSpace OS. It models physical, social, and neural strata to manage heavy-tailed innovations and ensure system stability under chaotic conditions.

## Core Components
1. **Stochastic Harness**: Generates governed Gaussian and Cauchy perturbations for stress-testing and simulation.
2. **MPW-FL Engine**: The Multiplicity-Prime Weighted Fuzzy Logic component maps probabilistic boundaries and constraints across the system's execution space.
3. **Spectral Admissibility Checker**: Enforces Lyapunov stability limits and ensures that multi-fiber interactions do not result in unbounded or catastrophic resonance.

## Invariants
- Innovation generators must accurately bound extreme Cauchy distributions according to the Λₘ-Lawfulness contract.
- Physical fibers (e.g., the 3-cycle Laplacian) must remain strictly constrained within verifiable bounds.
- System feedback loops mapping across ontological strata must pass spectral admissibility checks before propagation.
