# ADR-004: Perturbation Families for Metallurgical Fatigue

**Status:** PROPOSED  
**Date:** 2026-05-19  
**Author:** Gemini CLI Agent  
**Dependency:** ADR-001  

## Context
The Exploder requires a structured way to apply stress to the scalar core, specifically for the metallurgical fatigue substrate.

## Decision
We define an extensible `PerturbationFamily` interface. Initial families include:
- `amplitude_ramp`: Linear or exponential increase in stress amplitude.
- `frequency_shift`: Perturbation of the cyclic frequency ω.
- `phase_shift`: Sudden or gradual shifts in the stress phase.
- `timing_jitter`: Stochastic variations in perturbation timing.
- `drag_spike`: Artificial injection of kinematic drag to test recovery.
- `dual_symbolic_numeric`: Cross-domain stress involving both numerical dynamics and symbolic metadata.

## Rules
- Perturbations must stay within `expected_bounds` defined in the family.
- All perturbations must be deterministic given a fixed seed.

## Consequences
- Standardizes stress testing. Enables comparison between different Exploder runs.
