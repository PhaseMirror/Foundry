# 3-Categorical Meta-Recursion

**Version 1.0 – 2026-07-04**

## Positioning in the Stack

This layer sits at the **meta-level** above the 2-category treatment of recursion. It internalizes the *learning, transformation, and optimization of recursion rules themselves* as first-class citizens of the theory.

- **0-cells**: Prime signatures \(e \in \mathbf{Sig}\)
- **1-cells**: Graded prime-monomial matrices / tensor operations (PMat)
- **2-cells**: Certified recursive rewrites / PIRTM unfoldings / lawful contraction sequences
- **3-cells**: Meta-recursive transformations between 2-cells (or families of 2-cells)

A 3-cell \(\alpha : \beta \Rightarrow \gamma\) exists precisely when there is a signature-preserving, prime-power-stable, CSC-budget-respecting, Phase-Mirror-compatible, c-monotonicity-preserving way to transform the recursion strategy encoded by the 2-cell \(\beta\) into the one encoded by \(\gamma\).

Examples of 3-cells:
- Changing from prime-step recursion to prime-branching or Goldilocks-style gating
- Learning or updating a CRMF resonance predicate while preserving all lower invariants
- Transforming one certified contraction plan into another via meta-optimization
- Applying a higher-order Phase Mirror reflection to an entire family of recursive derivations

## Core Definition

We work in a (strict or weak) **tricategory** whose:
- 0-cells are prime signatures,
- 1-cells are morphisms in the prime-monomial matrix category (PMat),
- 2-cells are certified recursive steps (sequences of lawful 1-cell operations that preserve net signature and prime-power structure),
- 3-cells are meta-recursive transformations: natural transformations or rewrites between 2-cells that themselves respect all lower-layer invariants (including adelic c-monotonicity and Phase Mirror dissonance non-increase).

Vertical composition of 3-cells sequences meta-recursive steps. Horizontal composition tensors independent meta-recursive processes. The compact-closed structure (cups, caps, yanking) and Phase Mirror duality lift coherently to the 3-level.
