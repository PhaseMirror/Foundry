# ADR-107: Multiplicity-MT Central Tension Integration

## Status
Proposed (7-day horizon; 30-day deployment target)

## Context
Multiplicity Theory (MT) supplies structural analogies — prime-indexed multiplicity atoms, multiplicity-distribution (formation ↔ distribution) duality, Level-3 Euler factors — but operates at Level 1–2 or conjectural Level 3. Mapping MT onto the F1 substrate renames the open positivity (the Hodge index on `Spec ℤ ×_{𝔽₁} Spec ℤ`) without constructing the surface or proving signature `(1, ρ−1)` on `H^⊥`. This produces a **productive central tension**: high-resolution spectral resonance docking vs. verifiable multiplicity invariants under the ACE budget.

## Decision
Treat the MT–F1 tension as a **governed integration point**, not a solved bridge:

1. **Productive contradiction acknowledged.** The Weil reconstruction (band-limited, `L=20`, KS-validated gaps at `p > 0.05`) provides RH-neutral falsifiability and spectral resonance docking. Without explicit binding to RSL, CVK prime-gating, thickness metric, and twin-loop pre-execution gates, it remains **formal mathematics** — not admissible L0 substrate for governance.
2. **Explicit binding required (7-day target).**
   - Prime-power sum as RSL transition via `M(S)` functor.
   - CSL projector defaults to strict contraction `c < 1` under prime-indexed support (Aubrey 56-config eigenmode gate).
   - Resonance-gain (β/γ Hamiltonian mapping) exceptions logged to `⊥_R` with WORM rollback — never default.
   - Thickness metric `G(ψ) = Σ_p g(p)‖Π_p ψ‖²` verified non-expansion under CVK.
3. **The MT multiplicity functor as candidate divisor class.** Multiplicity atoms on κ-fibers (testable via κ + cycle profile) are a **structural factor** in characteristic 1 — testable but not yet proven to supply the ample class or Rosati-type positivity. This is a candidate, not a claim.
4. **Ahmad trace + Sedona Spine grammar refinement.** Dispatch Ahmad trace to validate AVP → RSL `⊥_R` with KS-validated thickness. Tree-sitter successor predicate + `M(S)` bound to CVK contraction.
5. **Governance artifact.** `Prime/adr/ADR-107-MT-Central-Tension.md` (this record) governs the integration. No further MT→F1 mapping may bypass the CVK harness or the honesty audit.
