\section*{1. Gauge sector: covariance and prime-weight action}
Non-abelian covariant derivatives and curvature:
\begin{align*}
D_\mu &= \partial_\mu - i g A_\mu^a T_a,\\
F_{\mu\nu} &= \partial_\mu A_\nu - \partial_\nu A_\mu - i g [A_\mu,A_\nu].
\end{align*}
Prime-indexed, unit-consistent gauge action with normalized weights $w_p\ge 0$, $\sum_p w_p=1$:
\begin{equation*}
\mathcal L_{\mathrm{gauge}} = -\frac{1}{4}\sum_{p\in\mathbb P} w_p\, \mathrm{Tr}\!\left(F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}\right),
\qquad w_p = \frac{p^{-\alpha}}{\sum_{q\in\mathbb P} q^{-\alpha}},\ \alpha>1.
\end{equation*}
This places the prime recursion in the couplings and preserves gauge invariance.
