## Context
General Relativity (GR) provides an exquisitely tested description of gravitation at macroscopic scales, but it suffers from singularities and lacks a microscopic quantum‑geometric foundation. The **Multiplicity Theory** initiative introduces a *prime‑indexed recursive* framework that:

* embeds GR as the macro‑limit of a discrete quantum geometry,

The ADR therefore **only transforms** engine‑computed facts (e.g. risk‑level, preservation alerts) into legal narratives; it does **not** re‑interpret risk levels.
