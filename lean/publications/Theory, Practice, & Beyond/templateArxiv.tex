Mathematically, a number \( N \) decomposed into prime factors is expressed as:
\begin{equation}
    N = p_1^{e_1} p_2^{e_2} \dots p_k^{e_k}, \quad p_i \text{ prime}
\end{equation}
where \( e_i \) are the exponent weights defining structural complexity.
