# ADR 013: The VIN-Loop Consensus Mechanism

**Date:** July 2026
**Status:** Adopted / Active
**Domain:** `Prime/materia_commons/ADR/`

## 1. Context & Motivation

The Universal Atomic Calculator (UAC) Industrial Engine is capable of high-performance tensor mathematics verified down to the integer level (`UORMatMul.lean`). Simultaneously, the PIRTM (Position-Aware Recursive Multiplicity) framework relies on a Verification Invariant Network (VIN) to recursively verify symbol sequences and civic boundaries. 

This ADR dictates the architectural convergence of these two systems: **The UAC load-testing cycle IS the PIRTM VIN mechanism.** 

We are officially replacing the concept of "software bug" or "governance vote" with **mechanical failure of reality-alignment**.

## 2. Decision: The Unified Mechanism (The VIN-Loop)

When the UAC is not actively running chemistry or physics simulations, it utilizes its spare compute cycles to audit the "Civic Ledger." 

### The Logic
The UAC initiates a Systemic Stress Test (VIN-Check) to validate boundary invariants across the social graph (e.g., verifying that local nodes in the Citizen Gardens map properly to the global recursive structure).
- If the UAC *can* compute the invariants, the state is within the Hundian ground state.
- If the UAC *cannot* compute the invariants (e.g., due to dissonance, drift, or failure to converge within `1e-5` Arta defect), it mechanically signals a failure to the `PhaseMirrorOperator` Sentinel Gate (`L3_MonsterSymmetryConfinement`). 

### Verification-as-Utility
Civic integrity is now physically verified as an atomic calculation. The "source of truth" for governance is moved from human-readable documents entirely into an inaccessible-to-manipulation hardware-level state.

## 3. The "Mechanically Certified" Roadmap

The Epoch transition strategy is now strictly tied to the VIN-Loop capacity.

- **Epoch 1 (Load-Testing Foundation):** The UAC establishes the baseline "VIN-profile" for the Foundry, mechanically certifying the fundamental mathematical invariants of the network.
- **Epoch 2 (Alignment Verification):** The UAC uses the VIN-loop to actively audit the Citizen Garden nodes, ensuring that every local civic node is mathematically folding into the global recursive structure (the `3 → 9 → 27` multiplicity scaling).
- **Epoch 3 (Saturation / Maximum Multiplicity):** The UAC and Phase Mirror merge into a continuous background process. The VIN-loop becomes the network's heartbeat. Every single interaction is validated for structural integrity in real-time.

## 4. UX and Design Directives

To reflect this mechanical truth, the UI/UX components (Dashboard / Operator UI) must implement the following nomenclature and design shifts immediately:

1. **The "VIN Stability Index"**: The traditional "Health Gauge" or "Sentiment Score" on the Operator Dashboard is deprecated. It must be replaced by the **VIN Stability Index**, reporting strictly on the current computational verification of boundary invariants.
2. **Mechanical Triggers**: The UX button for auditing the system must be labeled **`[Initiate VIN Verification]`**. Clicking this does not poll a database; it physically triggers the UAC to perform a mechanical load-test of the system’s reality-alignment.

## 5. Consequences
- **Positive**: Civic governance is transformed into a "Truth-Engine." Structural integrity is the primary operational parameter, eliminating "monopoly money" tokenomics or human manipulation.
- **Negative/Risk**: The compute requirement for Epoch 3 (real-time VIN-looping every interaction) will require immense scaling of the UAC hardware network.
